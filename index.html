WiSeCom Lab : Wireless Sensing and Communication Lab

Device Free WiFi Sensing (DWFS)

Recently DFWS has gained attention due to its low cost and penetration power. It is unobtrusive, ubiquitous, and privacy protected. It works on the principle of observed variations CSI (channel state information) values, due to change in the transmission medium. Machine learning techniques are used to recognize patterns in CSI values in different environmental conditions which then can be used as a sensing apparatus.
